# DoDuck Go 🦆⚖️

**Don’t be a quack.**

DoDuck Go is a guided complaint-triage assistant for Québec administrative judge ethics complaints. It helps users understand whether their issue may belong in the Conseil de la justice administrative (CJA) process and prepares a cleaner filing checklist.

## What it does

- Screens CJA fit and appeal red flags
- Collects key facts, conduct details, and available evidence
- Generates a structured intake for OpenJustice-powered analysis

## Built for

Montreal AI x Law Hackathon 2026 — Team #16

DoDuck Go provides legal information and triage support only. It does not decide admissibility, file complaints, or provide legal advice.
